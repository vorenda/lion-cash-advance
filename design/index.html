Everything you need to know about cash advances

A cash advance is a short-term loan designed to help you cover unexpected expenses until your next paycheck. You borrow a small amount (typically $100-$1,500) and repay it with your next paycheck, plus fees and interest as disclosed in your loan agreement.

If approved, most customers receive funds via direct deposit the same business day or next business day. The exact timing depends on your bank's processing times and when your application is approved.

To qualify, you typically need to: (1) Be at least 18 years old, (2) Have a steady source of income, (3) Have an active checking account, (4) Provide a valid phone number and email, (5) Be a resident of Florida or California.

Fees and APR vary based on loan amount, term, and your state. All rates and fees are disclosed clearly before you sign your loan agreement. Florida and California have specific regulations that cap rates and fees - we comply fully with all state lending laws. You will see your exact APR and total cost before accepting any loan.

Important Loan Information & Disclosures

APR Disclosure

The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for cash advances varies based on loan amount, term, and state regulations. APRs typically range from 200% to 600%. For example: A $500 loan with a 14-day term at 400% APR would cost approximately $577.40 to repay (including $77.40 in interest and fees). All rates and fees will be clearly disclosed before you accept any loan.

State Licensing

Florida: Lion Cash Advance is licensed by the Florida Office of Financial Regulation. Florida law caps payday loan amounts at $500 with terms between 7-31 days. Maximum fees are regulated by state law.

California: Lion Cash Advance is licensed under the California Deferred Deposit Transaction Law. California law caps payday loan amounts at $300 with maximum fees of 15% of the loan amount ($45 max).

Consumer Protections

You have the right to rescind this loan by 5:00 PM on the next business day. You may repay the loan early without penalty. We do not offer loan renewals or rollovers in states where prohibited. Late payments may result in additional fees and could negatively impact your credit score.

Important Warnings

Cash advances are designed for short-term financial needs, not long-term solutions. Borrowing more than you can afford to repay can lead to financial hardship. Consider all options before taking out a cash advance loan. Free financial counseling is available through the National Foundation for Credit Counseling at www.nfcc.org.
